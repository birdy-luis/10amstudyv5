üéØ The Big Picture: What is Edge Score?

What is Edge Score?

Edge Score is a single number (0-100) that tells you how HIGH QUALITY a trade setup is BEFORE you enter.

It's like a confidence rating for a potential trade opportunity.

Edge Score Ranges

0-40: Terrible setup, don't trade
40-60: Weak setup, mostly skip
60-75: Okay setup, trade only with extra signals
75-85: Good setup, enter with confidence
85-100: Excellent setup, enter aggressively

Why Do We Need Edge Score?

WITHOUT Edge Score (Most Traders):

  • "Umm, it looks good"
  • "I have a feeling about this one"
  • "My gut says yes"

This is GUESSING. You lose money guessing.

WITH Edge Score (Professional Traders):

  • "Score = 78. Check the formula."
  • "78 ‚â• 75, so I ENTER."
  • "Reason: High speed + good probe + solid consistency + normal efficiency"

This is CALCULATING. You make money calculating.

üìä Component 3: CONSISTENCY SCORE (30% Weight)

Definition

Consistency Score measures HOW RELIABLE this pattern/probe type is.

Consistency is asking: "Based on HISTORICAL DATA, does this setup usually work?"

It's about pattern reliability, not about the current setup.

Three Categories

üèÜ HIGH Consistency (Score = 90/100)

Definition: This setup pattern works 85%+ of the time historically.

Meaning: When you see this pattern in the past, it reversed and hit targets 85+ times out of 100.

Why it matters: HIGH consistency means the pattern is PROVEN. You have data backing you up.

‚úì MEDIUM Consistency (Score = 78/100)

Definition: This setup pattern works 60-85% of the time historically.

Meaning: Sometimes works, sometimes doesn't. Solid but not bulletproof.

Why it matters: MEDIUM consistency means it's worth trading, but consider extra confirmation signals.

‚ö†Ô∏è LOW Consistency (Score = 60/100)

Definition: This setup pattern works <60% of the time historically.

Meaning: It works sometimes, but it's unreliable. Coin-flip territory.

Why it matters: LOW consistency means be CAUTIOUS. Only trade with extra confirmation signals.

Level Score Historical Win Rate Interpretation
HIGH 90 85%+ Proven pattern

üö® Common Mistakes to Avoid

Mistake 1: Miscalculating Speed

The Error: You think a 3-minute extreme is FAST (92 score), when it's actually NORMAL (80 score).

Your calculation: Edge = 85 (using FAST 92)
Actual should be: Edge = 78 (using NORMAL 80)

How to Prevent: Use a timer religiously. Don't estimate. Write it down.

Without adjustment: Position = 1.0x
